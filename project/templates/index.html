NCC MOTO

The need for having motto for the Corps was discussed in the 11th Central Advisory Committee (CAC) meeting held on 11 Aug 1978. The mottos suggested were “Duty and Discipline”; “Duty, Unity and Discipline”; “Duty and Unity”; “Unity and Discipline”. The final decision for selection of “Unity and Discipline” as motto for the NCC was taken in the 12th CAC meeting held on 12 Oct 1980.

PURPOSE

The National Cadet Corps is a vibrant organization with pool of well-motivated and distinguished boys and girls. It plays a dominant role in nation building and imbibing selfless services, discipline and leadership among youth. NCC Cadets can join Defence services. It instils in the cadets a sense of commitment to the values of National Integration, Nationalism and secularism. These qualities would not only make the youth as responsible citizens, but also help in achieving the vision of developed India It plays commendable role in grooming the cadets both physically and mentally. The well-designed curriculum of National Cadet Corps, consisting of myriad training, adventure, cultural and social activities is of immense value to the young generation, enabling them to take on the challenges of the modern world.
